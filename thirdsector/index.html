Services for third sector organisations

Increase your impact and resilience

Sharpening your strategy

Do you want to sharpen your focus to ensure you continue to meet the needs of your service users and expectations of funders?

Whatever the complexity of your organisation’s challenges, I can help you gain clarity and focus. I will support you to identify strategic options – and decide how to prioritise your resources.

I have extensive experience in developing strategy using a collaborative approach.

Working with you and your teams I help you to develop or refresh your strategy. I use targeted interventions that enthuse and create momentum.

Designing outstanding service user experiences

I started my career as a User Experience Designer and I’m still passionate about designing great experiences.

I will help you to take stock of your service user experience and look through the eyes of your users. I can facilitate a collaborative process that engages your staff and stakeholders. At the end you will have a pragmatic and impactful action plan.

Let’s discuss how I can support you

Managing change with stakeholder buy-in

I can help you to design and manage change which stakeholders will support.

Tapping into my wide-ranging experience I help you challenge existing ways of thinking. I love creative problem-solving and will help you to arrive at solutions which work for you and your stakeholders.

You will benefit from my extensive experience in designing effective co-creation and consultation approaches. I will assist you to develop an effective and pragmatic approach that gets buy-in from your stakeholders.

As an experienced project manager I can also support you to develop an operational plan for successfully implementing change.
