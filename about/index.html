COACHING  |  FACILITATION  |  CONSULTANCY


Catherine Brys PhD(Eng) MBA



Catherine has many strings on her bow. Over her 20+ year career, she has held roles as varied as User Experience Designer, Change Manager & Project Manager and Strategy Analyst. She has worked for large corporates, SMEs, the public sector and academia. As a coach, facilitator and consultant she has worked with individuals and teams in the private and third sector. Catherine is driven by helping people feel fulfilled and achieve more impact in what they do.

Catherine excels at helping people see clarity in complex situations. Her scientific mindset allows her to systematically but quickly diagnose issues and unearth root causes. Catherine is able to guide clients to develop a pragmatic action plan that will help them realise their vision for success.

Catherine has an MBA, with specialism Strategy, from Strathclyde Business School. She has a degree and PhD in Engineering/Physics from the University of Ghent, Belgium. She is a trained workplace coach and facilitator. She is also qualified and accredited as a good relations practitioner (by the Centre for Good Relations) and as a negotiation and conflict resolution professional (Postgraduate Certificate in Mediation, Conflict Resolution and Negotiation from Strathclyde University). In her spare time she volunteers as a Lead mediator at Strathclyde University Mediation Clinic.

  • "Workshop designed and facilitated to focus on solving the problem in hand"
  • "Helicopter view to cut to the chase to understand where blockages are"
  • "Ability to ‘cut through the noise’ and identify the key issues"

When coaching individuals to feel more fulfilled in their working life, Catherine uses deep listening and gentle challenging to help them uncover what they really want. She guides them to develop clarity and decide on an achievable course of action.

In her work with businesses and third sector organisations, Catherine encourages leaders to formulate an ambitious vision for the future. Catherine's clients appreciate her strategic thinking and how she keeps them focused on their vision for success. She understands that for change to be successful it needs to be co-created with staff and stakeholders, and she is an experienced facilitator. Her extensive experience as a Change Manager means that she has a treasure trove of insights and practical experience she can tap into. Catherine focuses on short interventions that enthuse and create momentum, and result in pragmatic actions that clients can execute.
