Improving your profitability and resilience

I specialise in targeted interventions that enthuse and create momentum. I work with you and your staff to develop a pragmatic action plan that creates impactful and sustainable change.

Sharpening your strategy

A key factor in your long-term profitability is how your products or services meet your customers’ needs better than the competition. Now and in the future.

  • What is your long-term vision of success?
  • Do you know what attracts customers to your business? Which customers you miss out on?
  • Have you identified the vulnerabilities in your competitive advantage?
  • Do you scan for early signals of change that may disrupt your offering?

If your business is in the start-up phase, I can help you to develop your strategy using a structured and flexible approach. If you business is established, I can guide you in planning and executing a strategy refresh. I will also help you put the mechanism in place to keep your strategy up-to-date in the future.

“I’ve worked with Catherine for over 5 years. Catherine combines a strategic mindset with a highly structured and analytical approach. This makes her a very valuable asset in developing as well as reviewing strategies and new ideas. She’s acted as a great sounding board for some of my own thinking and can be counted on to offer new and valuable insights.”
Ewan Mearns, Senior Manager, Strategy & Sectors at Scottish Enterprise

Making your customer experience top-notch

Customer experience has become the main differentiator from the competition. Clients have increasingly high expectations, and share their experiences – positive or negative – on social media.

  • Do you know how good your customer experience is? How good it could be?
  • Do all your teams appreciate their role in delivering excellent customer experience?
  • Do you engage deeply with customers to gain insights?

I can guide you to take stock of your customer experience in a structured way. I can also facilitate discussions that will allow you to co-develop a customer experience vision together with your staff so that they feel ownership. I will support you and your teams to draw up a practical action plan, and an approach to monitor progress towards the vision.

Feedback from participants at a recent Customer Experience Excellence workshop for a law firm:

“It made me think about the client’s perspective and how things look from the other side.”

Improving staff satisfaction

Staff feeling fulfilled at work improves their satisfaction and loyalty, which contribute to productivity and profitability. Two key elements of staff fulfilment are the feeling of making a meaningful contribution to a worthwhile goal and being part of a high-performing team.

  • How fulfilled do your staff feel? To what extent do they feel that they are contributing to a worthwhile goal?
  • Do your staff realise how they contribute to the success of your business?
  • Is profitability at the front of every employee’s mind?
  • Do your staff feel supported and part of a high-performing team working towards the same purpose?

I can bring an outside perspective and help you consider where there may be room for improvement. I can help you identify blind spots and look at staff fulfilment anew. I will support you and your teams to articulate a joint vision and develop approaches that will help individuals thrive and teams work together effectively.

Optimising your profitability holistically

To optimise your profitability you need to analyse how individual profitability factors interconnect and influence each other. Soft factors that drive or hinder will become apparent, such as individual and team incentives and team dynamics. Inefficiencies in business processes will also be discovered.

  • Do you have a clear view of the factors that help or hinder your profitability, and how they may reinforce each other?
  • Do you know where the key profitability drivers or blockers are?
  • Do you have a structured plan to address issues?

I can help you map out your profitability factors and get deep insight into how they interact. I can help you prioritise actions so you achieve maximum impact. If needed I can review business processes, both internal and client-facing, to pinpoint further inefficiencies. Using rigorous analysis I can quickly identify issues and help you to draw up a pragmatic action plan that will lead to sustainable change.
