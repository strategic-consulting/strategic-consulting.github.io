Services at a glance

INDIVIDUALS

Achieve your goals – and enjoy the journey

Work-related individual coaching for professionals of all ages

I can help with any work issue, for example:

  • Work-life balance and fulfilment at work
  • Personal effectiveness
  • Leadership challenges
  • Dealing with conflict
  • Research strategy (for academics/researchers)

Coaching can be a one-off session or several sessions at times that suit you.

Read more about coaching for individuals


TEAMS, BOARDS & PARTNERSHIPS

Work better together

Design & facilitation of workshops and constructive discussions for diverse teams, groups and partnerships, in particular:

  • Improving team dynamics
  • Developing a shared vision and action plan
  • Working more effectively together

I can design a one-off workshop or work with you over a longer period of time.

Read more about how I can help your team, Board or partnership


COMPANIES

Increase your profitability and resilience

Consultancy, coaching and facilitation for micro and small to medium-sized companies, with a focus on:

  • Strategy development
  • Excellence in Customer Experience
  • Resolution of business-to-business issues
  • Improving business-related working relationships, e.g. between partners or co-founders

Read more about how your company can benefit from my support


THIRD SECTOR

Increase your impact and resilience

Consultancy, coaching and facilitation for small and medium-sized third-sector organisations, with a focus on:

  • Strategy development
  • Excellence in Service User Experience
  • Managing change
