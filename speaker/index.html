REACH YOUR POTENTIAL – AND ENJOY THE JOURNEY


Speaker & facilitator

For webinars and workshops

I specialise in engaging, thought-provoking events that give your attendees the tools to take action, either:

  • Fully bespoke for your team or organisation only (closed event).
  • Public and tailored to your target audience (open event).

I use a systematic approach to ensure that your objectives and the interests of your audience are met (read more about my approach).

Topics I have presented on:

  • Customer experience excellence
  • Service user experience
  • Building good relationships – preventing and addressing friction and conflict
  • Becoming more effective as a team
  • Strategy development
  • From vision and values to action plan

In my workshops and talks I tend to use a mixture of food for thought, discussion, sharing of best practice and practical tools. I usually include time for attendees to learn from each other and to draw up an (individual) action plan.
